# Billing Policy
**InspectPro — Property Inspection Software**
_Last Updated: March 2026 | Version 1.0_

---

This Billing Policy explains how InspectPro handles subscriptions, payments, cancellations, refunds, and account access. It applies to all company accounts that purchase a subscription through our website.

This policy forms part of our [Terms of Use](./TERMS_OF_USE.md) and [End User License Agreement](./EULA.md). In the event of any conflict, this policy applies to billing matters.

---

## 1. Free Trial

- Every new company account receives a **10-day free trial** from the date the account is created and the first device is linked to the account.
- No payment is required during the trial period. Full subscription features are available.
- The trial applies once per company account. Adding employees during the trial does not extend it.
- At the end of the trial period, your account will be charged using the payment method on file.
- If no payment method is on file when the trial ends, your account will enter **Restricted Mode** (see §6).

---

## 2. Subscription Plans

- InspectPro subscriptions are billed on a **per-seat, monthly** basis.
- Pricing is in **New Zealand Dollars (NZD), exclusive of GST**.
- Current pricing is available at [inspectpro.co.nz/pricing](https://inspectpro.co.nz/pricing).
- All seats within a company account are billed on the same monthly cycle.
- We reserve the right to change pricing with **30 days' written notice**. Continued use of the Service after the notice period constitutes acceptance of the new pricing.

---

## 3. Billing & Payment

- Subscriptions are billed on the same day each month, corresponding to the date your subscription started.
- Payment is processed securely by **Stripe**. We do not store or have access to your payment card details.
- You can manage your billing, update payment methods, download invoices, and change your plan at any time through the **[Stripe Customer Portal](#)**.
- GST invoices are available from the Stripe Customer Portal.
- For billing questions or assistance, contact us at **billing@aiconsult.co.nz**.

---

## 4. Cancellation

- You may cancel your subscription at any time through the Stripe Customer Portal, or by contacting us at billing@aiconsult.co.nz.
- Cancellation takes effect at the **end of your current billing period**. You retain full access until then.
- **No refund is issued for the remaining days in the current billing period.**
- After your subscription ends, your account enters Restricted Mode (see §6).

---

## 5. Failed Payments

- If a payment fails, Stripe will automatically retry over a **7-day period** using smart retry logic.
- During the retry window, your account retains full access.
- We will notify your company administrator by email on the first payment failure and again before access is restricted.
- If payment is not successfully collected after 7 days, your account will enter **Restricted Mode** (see §6).
- To resolve a failed payment: update your payment method in the Stripe Customer Portal or contact billing@aiconsult.co.nz.
- Once a payment is successfully processed, full access is restored immediately.

---

## 6. Restricted Mode

Restricted Mode applies when:
- Your free trial ends without a payment method on file
- Your subscription is cancelled and the billing period has ended
- A failed payment has not been resolved within the 7-day grace period
- Your account is suspended for a Terms of Use violation

**In Restricted Mode:**
- No new inspections can be created
- Existing inspections and photos remain on your device (your data is stored locally — we do not delete it)
- Existing completed reports can still be viewed and exported as PDFs
- No new report exports for reports created after restriction began

**To restore full access:** Resubscribe through the Stripe Customer Portal or contact billing@aiconsult.co.nz.

---

## 7. Refunds

- **No refunds** are issued for partial subscription periods or unused trial days.
- Refunds required under the **Consumer Guarantees Act 1993 (New Zealand)** will be honoured where applicable.
- To request a refund or dispute a charge, contact **billing@aiconsult.co.nz within 30 days** of the charge. Include your account email and the charge date.
- We will respond to refund requests within 5 business days.

---

## 8. Termination by InspectPro

- We may restrict or terminate your account for: violation of our Terms of Use, suspected fraud or abuse, or non-payment after the 7-day grace period.
- For non-payment: accounts are restricted (not immediately terminated) after the grace period. Termination only occurs if the account remains unresolved.
- For serious violations (fraud, abuse, illegal activity): we may terminate your account immediately without prior notice.
- On termination: your subscription will not be renewed. Your locally stored data is not affected — we do not delete data from your device.
- To dispute a restriction or termination, contact billing@aiconsult.co.nz.

---

## 9. Your Rights Under New Zealand Law

Nothing in this Billing Policy limits or excludes any rights you have under:
- **Consumer Guarantees Act 1993 (NZ)**
- **Fair Trading Act 1986 (NZ)**

Where those Acts grant you rights, they apply regardless of what this policy states.

We may update this Billing Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified by:
- Email to your company administrator's registered address
- Notice within the App

Continued use of the Service after the effective date of changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
