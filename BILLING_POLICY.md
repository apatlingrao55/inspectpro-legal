Who This Policy Applies To\n\nDifferent rights and obligations apply depending on how you use InspectPro.\n\n**Consumer users** are individuals who acquire InspectPro primarily for personal use, or not principally in the course of a trade, business, or profession. Individual subscribers are presumed to be consumers under the Consumer Guarantees Act 1993 (CGA) unless they indicate otherwise. Consumer users have rights under the CGA that cannot be excluded or limited by this policy.\n\n**Business users** are persons or entities that acquire InspectPro in the course of carrying on a trade, business, or profession. Where both parties agree the acquisition is clearly for business purposes, certain CGA provisions may not apply (CGA s.43).\n\nIf you are unsure which category applies to your situation, contact us at billing@aiconsult.co.nz.\n\n---\n\n## 2. Consumer users are entitled to a full refund of the first charge if we cannot demonstrate that reasonable notice was provided before the charge was processed.\n\n**At the end of your trial:**\n- If you have **not** entered a payment method: paid features will be locked at the end of day 10. You can still view and export any inspections completed during the trial period at no charge.\n- If you have **entered a payment method** and activated a subscription: your chosen subscription will continue automatically from the date the trial ends. You will receive a reminder notification **at least 48 hours before** your first charge is processed.\n\n**What \"free\" means:** The trial period costs nothing. You will not be charged unless you explicitly choose to subscribe and provide a valid payment method. We will never charge you without clear advance notice.\n\n**Trial limitations:** The free trial is offered once per device or account. Creating additional accounts to obtain further trials may result in account suspension.\n\n---\n\n## 3. Billing & Payment\n\n- Subscriptions renew automatically on the same calendar day each month, corresponding to the date your subscription commenced.\n- Payment is processed securely by **Stripe**. InspectPro does not store or have access to your full payment card details.\n- You can manage your billing details, update your payment method, download invoices, and change your subscription plan at any time through the **Stripe Customer Portal** (accessible from within the App).\n- GST-inclusive tax invoices are available from the Stripe Customer Portal.\n- For billing questions or assistance, contact us at **billing@aiconsult.co.nz**.\n\n**Plan changes mid-cycle:** If you upgrade your plan during a billing period, the price difference will be charged on a pro-rata basis for the remaining days of that billing period. If you downgrade your plan, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing period.\n\n---\n\n## 5. Cancellation\n\n- You may cancel your subscription at any time through the Stripe Customer Portal, or by contacting us at billing@aiconsult.co.nz.\n- Cancellation takes effect at the **end of your current billing period**. You retain full subscription access until that date.\n- After your billing period ends, your account enters **Restricted Mode** (see \u00a77).\n\n**Note for consumer users:** Cancellation before the end of a billing period does not automatically entitle you to a partial refund of the remaining days \u2014 however, if InspectPro has failed to meet a consumer guarantee under the CGA, you may be entitled to a remedy. See \u00a78.\n\n---\n\n## 6. Failed Payments\n\n- If a payment fails, Stripe will automatically retry over a **7-day period**.\n- During the retry window, your account retains full access.\n- We will notify your account's registered email address on the first payment failure and again before access is restricted.\n- If payment is not successfully collected after 7 days, your account will enter **Restricted Mode** (see \u00a77).\n- To resolve a failed payment: update your payment method in the Stripe Customer Portal, or contact billing@aiconsult.co.nz.\n- Once payment is successfully processed, full access is restored immediately.\n\n---\n\n## 7. Restricted Mode\n\nYour account enters Restricted Mode when:\n- Your free trial ends and you have not activated a subscription\n- Your subscription is cancelled and the billing period has ended\n- A failed payment has not been resolved within the 7-day grace period\n- Your account is suspended for a Terms of Use violation\n\n**What happens in Restricted Mode:**\n- No new inspections can be created\n- Existing inspections and photos remain accessible on your device (data is stored locally)\n- Existing completed reports can still be viewed and exported as PDFs\n- New report exports for reports created *after* restriction commenced are not available\n- Account data is retained for **90 days** from the date of restriction, after which server-side account data may be deleted (see \u00a79). If you explicitly request account deletion during this period, server-side data will be deleted within **30 days** of a verified deletion request (see \u00a79)\n\n**Restoring full access:** Resubscribe through the Stripe Customer Portal or contact billing@aiconsult.co.nz.\n\n---\n\n## 8. Refunds and Consumer Rights\n\n### 8.1 Consumer Rights Under the CGA (Consumer Users)\n\nIf you are a **consumer user** (see \u00a71), you have rights under the Consumer Guarantees Act 1993. These rights cannot be excluded or limited by this policy. They include:\n\n- The right to a remedy (which may include a refund) if InspectPro fails to meet the consumer guarantees \u2014 for example, if the service is not fit for its purpose, not of acceptable quality, or not as described.\n- If the failure is minor, we are entitled to remedy it (e.g., by fixing the issue). If the failure is substantial or cannot be fixed, you may be entitled to cancel the service and receive a refund.\n\nIf you believe InspectPro has failed to meet a consumer guarantee, please contact us at billing@aiconsult.co.nz. We will assess your situation and respond within 5 business days. Your CGA rights exist regardless of our general refund policy below.\n\n### 8.2 General Refund Policy\n\nFor **business users** and for situations not covered by the CGA:\n\n- No refund is issued for partial billing periods when you cancel a subscription.\n- For **business users** (where the CGA has been excluded under s 43): no refund is issued for change of mind after a subscription payment has been processed. For **consumer users**, your refund rights under the Consumer Guarantees Act 1993 are not affected by this clause.\n- No refund is issued for unused days during a billing period.\n\nFor **all users**, where InspectPro experiences a prolonged material service failure (for example, core functionality being unavailable for more than 24 hours through our fault), we may at our discretion provide a pro-rata credit or partial refund for the affected period.\n\n### 8.3 How to Request a Refund\n\nContact **billing@aiconsult.co.nz** within **30 days** of the charge. Please include:\n- Your account email address\n- The charge date and amount\n- A description of the issue or concern\n\nWe will respond within **5 business days**.\n\n---\n\n## 9. Your Data\n\n**Data stored on your device:** Inspection data, photos, and completed reports are stored locally on your device. InspectPro does not delete this data as a result of subscription changes, cancellation, or account restriction.\n\n**Data stored by InspectPro (server-side):** Your account information (email address, subscription history, billing records) is stored on our servers.\n\n- During an active subscription or Restricted Mode period: your server-side account data is retained.\n- **Where you explicitly request account deletion:** server-side account data will be deleted within **30 days** of a verified deletion request, in accordance with our Privacy Policy.\n- **After account termination or expiry of Restricted Mode (no deletion request made):** server-side account data is retained for up to **90 days** to allow for reactivation or data export requests. After 90 days, account data may be permanently deleted.\n- You may request export or deletion of your server-side data at any time by contacting privacy@aiconsult.co.nz. We will process your request in accordance with the Privacy Act 2020.\n\n---\n\n## 10.
