## 6. Your Data and User-Generated Content

### 6.1 Ownership

You retain full ownership of all content you create using the App, including photos, inspection notes, and reports. We do not claim any ownership over your inspection content.

### 6.2 Local Storage

Inspection content is stored locally on your device as the only copy held within InspectPro. You are responsible for maintaining your own backups and for any loss of locally stored data.

### 6.3 Backup

**You are responsible for maintaining your own backups.**

**Manual Backup (user-initiated):** You can create a full backup at any time via **Settings → Data Management → Backup**. This generates a ZIP file containing all inspections, photos, and settings. You can restore from a backup via the same screen. Restoring from a backup replaces all existing inspection data on your device.

We also recommend using your device's built-in backup features (e.g. iCloud device backup) as an additional precaution. These are independent of InspectPro.

InspectPro does not automatically sync inspection data to our servers. Your inspection content is stored locally on your device only.

### 6.4 Temporary PDF Uploads

When you use the **Approval Workflow** or **Send to Client** features, finalised PDF reports are temporarily uploaded to Supabase Storage to enable those delivery flows:

- Approval PDFs are automatically deleted after **72 hours**
- Client delivery PDFs are automatically deleted after **7 days**

Outside of these user-initiated flows, we do not access or process your inspection content for any purpose.

### 6.5 Data on Termination

On account termination or subscription cancellation, your account data in Supabase will be deleted within **30 days** of the termination date. Pending PDF delivery files will be deleted on their automated schedule (72h / 7 days). Your locally stored data and any manual backup exports remain under your control and are not affected by account termination.
